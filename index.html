Food Information

Learn about food safety, storage, and nutrition

Dairy Products

Dairy products are excellent sources of calcium, protein, and other essential nutrients. However, they are highly perishable and should be consumed before their expiry date to avoid foodborne illnesses.

  • Store dairy products at 40°F (4°C) or below
  • Check expiry dates regularly
  • Discard any products that smell sour or have unusual texture

Fresh Fruits

Fresh fruits are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Consuming them regularly helps boost your immune system and overall health.

  • Store most fruits in the refrigerator to extend freshness
  • Keep bananas, avocados, and tomatoes at room temperature
  • Wash fruits thoroughly before consumption

Risks of Eating Expired Food

Consuming expired food can lead to foodborne illnesses caused by harmful bacteria, parasites, or viruses. Symptoms may include:

  • Nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea
  • Abdominal pain and cramps
  • Fever and dehydration

How EatWise Helps You

EatWise app helps you track the expiry dates of your food items and manage your inventory efficiently.

  • Scan QR codes on food items to add them to your inventory
  • Get notifications before items expire
  • Maintain a digital record of your food inventory
  • Reduce food waste and save money
